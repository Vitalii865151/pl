1 At the train station – buying a ticket

Customer: Good morning! I'd like a ticket to Kraków for tomorrow, please.
Clerk: Good morning! What time would you like to travel?
Customer: Preferably in the morning, around 9:00.
Clerk: We have a train at 9:15. The ticket costs 45 złoty.
Customer: One ticket, please.
Clerk: Here you go. Have a nice trip!

2 At the airport – baggage check-in

Employee: Good morning, may I see your passport and ticket?
Traveler: Of course, here you are.
Employee: Do you have any luggage to check in?
Traveler: Yes, one suitcase.
Employee: Please place it on the scale. The weight is fine.
Traveler: Thank you.
Employee: Please proceed to gate number 7. Have a nice flight!

3 At the hotel – check-in

Receptionist: Good morning! Do you have a reservation?
Guest: Yes, under the name Kowalski.
Receptionist: Here is your key. Room 305, on the third floor.
Guest: Thank you very much.
Receptionist: Have a pleasant stay.
Guest: Thank you!

4 On the bus – asking about the stop

Passenger: Excuse me, does this bus go to the city center?
Driver: Yes, it's the last stop.
Passenger: Thank you!
Driver: You're welcome.

5 At the tourist information – asking about attractions

Tourist: Good morning! What is worth seeing in this city?
Employee: I recommend the castle, the museum, and a walk by the river.
Tourist: Are guided tours available?
Employee: Yes, daily at 10:00 and 14:00.
Tourist: Great, thank you for the information!
Employee: You're welcome!
